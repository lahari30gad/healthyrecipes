Chickpea & Roasted Red Pepper Lettuce Wraps with Tahini Dressing

Nution Profile

  • Dairy-Free
  • Healthy Aging
  • Low-Sodium
  • Soy-Free
  • High-Fiber

Ingridients

  • ¼ cup tahini
  • ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • ¼ cup lemon juice (from 2 lemons)
  • ¾ teaspoon kosher salt
  • ½ teaspoon paprika
  • 2 (15 ounce) cans no-salt-added chickpeas, rinsed
  • ½ cup sliced jarred roasted red peppers, drained

Instructions

  • Step 1: Whisk tahini, oil, lemon zest, lemon juice, maple syrup, salt and paprika in a large bowl. Add chickpeas, peppers and shallots. Toss to coat.
  • Step 2: Divide the mixture among lettuce leaves (about 1/3 cup each). Top with almonds and parsley. Wrap the lettuce leaves around the filling and serve.
