Sriracha, Egg & Avocado Overnight Oats

Nutrition Profile

  • Dairy-Free
  • Healthy Aging
  • Low-Sodium
  • Soy-Free
  • High-Fiber

Ingridients

  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • ¾ cup water
  • 1 tablespoon onion
  • ¼ avocado, sliced
  • 2 cherry tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 large egg, fried
  • 1 teaspoon Sriracha

Instructions

  • Combine oats and water in a microwave-safe bowl. Microwave on High for 2 minutes.
  • Top with onion, avocado, tomatoes, egg, and Sriracha.
