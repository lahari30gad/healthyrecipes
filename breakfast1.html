Cinnamon Roll Overnight Oats

Nutrition Profile

  • Dairy-Free
  • Vegan
  • Vegetarian
  • Egg-Free
  • Gluten-Free

Ingridients

  • 2½ cups old-fashioned rolled oats (see Tip)
  • 2½ cups unsweetened nondairy milk, such as almond or coconut
  • 6 teaspoons light brown sugar
  • 1½ teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 ¼ teaspoons ground cinnamon

Instructions

  • Combine oats, milk, brown sugar, vanilla and cinnamon in a medium bowl. Divide between 2 bowls, cover and refrigerate for at least 8 hours and up to 12 hours.
  • Stir the oats and top with a dollop of yogurt, a drizzle of maple syrup and a sprinkle of cinnamon, if desired.
